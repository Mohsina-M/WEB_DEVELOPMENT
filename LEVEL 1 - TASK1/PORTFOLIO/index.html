Disease Predictor BOT

The main aim of the project is to identify health problems early, so doctors can help patients sooner and improve their quality of life. We start by asking questions about symptoms and medical history in a special interview. Then, we use machine learning techniques to examine the person physically. Finally, the project predicts the health issue to make healthcare prediction easier.

A New Galactic Swarm Optimization Algorithm Enhanced with Grey wolf optimizer for training for Artificial Neural Network

My research paper focuses on an innovative approach that combines the Galactic Swarm Optimization (GSO) algorithm with the Grey Wolf Optimizer (GWO) to enhance its performance. The proposed algorithm is employed in training a feed-forward Neural Network (FNN) and is evaluated through its ability to train an FNN for function approximation of benchmark function on optimization problems, to minimize the Mean Squared Error (MSE) between the targeted input and the desired output.
